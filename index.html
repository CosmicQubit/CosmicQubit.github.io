FAQ

Do you take commisions?

Depends. I mostly do all my work as a hobby and have other responsibilities beyond that. So my answer to commision requests depend on how free I am and the workload of the commision. But I am open to any suggestions people have. Just be aware that I won't necessarily do them since I have limited time and anything too work intensive is not possible. Also, I hate sculpting.
